# Bushfire Emergency Response Procedures

## Emergency Contact
- Emergency services: **000**
- State fire authority hotline: 1800 240 667

## Fire Danger Rating Levels
1. **Low-Moderate**: Normal operations. Monitor conditions.
2. **High**: Increased vigilance. Review evacuation routes.
3. **Very High**: Restrict field activities to essential operations only.
4. **Extreme**: Cease all non-emergency field operations. Activate evacuation plan.
5. **Catastrophic**: Full evacuation. Shelter in designated safe zones only.

## Ranger Response Protocol
1. Assess immediate danger to personnel and visitors
2. Contact emergency services (000) if fire is spotted
3. Activate park evacuation plan if fire danger is Very High or above
4. Secure wildlife monitoring equipment where safe to do so
5. Report to incident controller at park headquarters

## Evacuation Assembly Points
- Main visitor centre car park
- Ranger station compound
- Designated helicopter landing zones (see park map)

## Post-Fire Assessment
- Document fire extent using GPS coordinates
- Record wildlife casualties and habitat damage
- Assess infrastructure damage
- Begin rehabilitation planning within 48 hours
